Delicious Aroma Café

Project Overview

Paradiso Networx was chosen to revamp the technology setup for Delicious Aroma Café, focusing on atmosphere, efficiency, and seamless operations. Our team installed IP-based audio systems that allow customized background music in the lounge and terrace, setting the perfect mood throughout the day.

We also integrated a modern Point of Sale (POS) system to improve transaction speed and accuracy, ensuring that customer orders are processed without delays. The setup is managed via tablets, making the system easy to use and maintain.
